# TON Stories Concept

TON Stories is a Telegram-native advertising product concept focused on Telegram Stories placements.

The main idea is to reduce friction for advertisers who want to use Telegram Stories but do not want to coordinate every placement, creative step, status update, and publication detail manually.

## Core Idea

Advertisers describe what they want to promote, then receive a guided campaign flow oriented around Telegram communication. The product concept emphasizes clarity, speed, and practical promotion inside Telegram.

## Public Capabilities

- explain campaign goals in plain language
- coordinate Telegram Stories placements
- support advertiser communication in Telegram
- keep campaign progress understandable
- make Telegram Stories promotion more accessible

## Why Telegram Stories

Telegram Stories can work as a high-attention advertising format because the placement is native to Telegram behavior. For brands already using Telegram, Stories can add visibility without relying only on feed posts or external landing pages.

## Implementation Boundary

This document intentionally avoids technical architecture, private workflows, partner logic, prompt design, data schemas, and operational playbooks.
